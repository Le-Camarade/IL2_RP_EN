## A — Sortie Detail

**Mission:** Ground Hunt — motorised transport interdiction
**Take-off time:** 09:30
**Landing time:** ~12:15
**Base:** Volkel (B-80), Netherlands

## B — Narrative

Red Section (4× Spitfire Mk.IXe) took off from Volkel at 09:30. Assigned top cover: 373rd FG, P-47D-22. The escort did not appear over the objective area.

On approach to the Noord-Brabant sector (~10:50), a formation of four Fw 190s attacked out of the sun from above. Falstaff broke on radio call and engaged immediately.

**Engagement 1:** Falstaff pursued the first attacker and scored a direct hit on the cockpit. The aircraft lost control and spun in. Pilot presumed killed outright.

**Engagement 2:** Red 2 (Dusaussoy), having failed to break, was shot up by a second Fw 190. Falstaff turned back onto the attacker and landed a well-placed burst — both wings severed. Aircraft destroyed.

**Engagement 3:** Another allied aircraft (Red 3, Chevalier) was hit by a third Fw 190. Falstaff engaged and set the engine on fire. Aircraft destroyed.

**Engagement 4:** The fourth Fw 190 (D-9 variant — presumed leader) placed Falstaff in a pursuit. Barrel roll executed. The enemy aircraft crossed back in front. Destroyed by short-range burst.

At the end of the engagements, no further enemy aircraft remained in the sector. Falstaff rejoined Vitry and set course for base. The American escort appeared on the return leg.

Dusaussoy: last radio message — in difficulty. Crash-landing confirmed, ~50 km west of Volkel. Seriously wounded.
Chevalier: crash-landing confirmed, combat sector. Status under assessment.

Primary mission (convoy interdiction) not executed — flight engaged upon arrival over the area.
